Interactive Personal Finance Dashboard in Excel for tracking income, expenses, savings, and monthly financial trends.

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a fully interactive **Personal Finance Dashboard** built using Microsoft Excel. It consolidates income and expense data into a single view and provides structured analysis through dynamic visuals, enabling **data-driven financial planning and decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Track income and expenses efficiently
* Analyze category-wise spending patterns
* Monitor monthly financial performance
* Evaluate savings and financial health

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 🔹 KPI Cards (Top Section)

* **Total Income:** ₹4,77,154
* **Total Expense:** ₹2,23,425
* **Net Savings:** ₹2,17,025

**Formula:**
Net Savings = Total Income – Total Expense

👉 KPI cards are dynamically linked to Pivot Tables and update automatically.

---

### 🔹 Category-wise Expense Analysis

A bar chart showing expenses across categories:

* Food (Highest)
* Shopping (Second highest)
* Entertainment, Transportation, Health
* Fuel (Lowest)

**Insight:**
Spending is heavily concentrated in **Food and Shopping**, indicating potential areas for budget optimization.

---

### 🔹 Income vs Expense Comparison

A comparative chart displaying total income and total expenses.

**Insight:**
A clear gap between income and expense indicates **strong financial stability and savings potential**.

---

### 🔹 Monthly Analysis

A month-wise comparison of income and expenses.

**Observations:**

* Income remains relatively stable
* Expenses fluctuate across months
* Income consistently exceeds expenses

**Insight:**
Maintains a **positive savings trend throughout the year**.

---

### 🔹 Income Source Distribution

Pie chart showing:

* Salary (Major contributor)
* Rent Income (Minor contributor)

**Insight:**
Primary dependency is on salary, while rental income provides additional support.

---

### 🔹 Interactive Month Filter

* Month slicer (January–December)
* Filters entire dashboard dynamically
* Enables focused monthly analysis

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

* **Savings Rate:** ~45%
* **Highest Expense Category:** Food
* **Second Highest Expense:** Shopping
* **Primary Income Source:** Salary
* **Financial Trend:** Consistent positive savings
